WAVES Surfing School

Founded in 2014, WAVES Surfing School provides classes for beginning and seasoned surfers. WAVES Surfing School is accredited by the National Instructors and Surf Schools Association. Our trainers are all certified with the SSA as well as Red Cross and First Aid. Classes can be group or private, and customized to individual needs.
